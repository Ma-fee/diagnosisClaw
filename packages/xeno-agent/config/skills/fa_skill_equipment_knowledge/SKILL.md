# Equipment Knowledge

## Core Principle
Provide accurate, technical information about equipment systems, components, principles, and maintenance standards.

## Knowledge Domains

### 1. Hydraulic Systems
- Pumps, motors, valves, cylinders
- Circuit logic and flow paths
- Pressure setting and relief principles

### 2. Electrical Systems
- Power circuits, control circuits, CAN bus
- Sensors, solenoids, controllers
- Wiring diagrams and connector pinouts

### 3. Mechanical Systems
- Engines, transmissions, gears, bearings
- Structural components and wear limits
- Fasteners and torque specifications

## Application Rules

- **Accuracy**: Always verify values against manuals.
- **Context**: Explain *why* a component is used or how it works.
- **Safety**: Highlight safety implications of components (e.g., accumulators store energy).

## Usage
Use this skill to answer "How does it work?", "What is this?", "Where is it located?" questions, often supporting the `maintenance_guidance` skill.
